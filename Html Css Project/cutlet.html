VEGETABLE CUTLET


Ingrediants


2 large potatoes, peeled and diced
1 carrot, peeled and diced
½ cup fresh green beans, trimmed and snapped
¼ cup frozen green peas
1 ½ cups bread crumbs
½ teaspoon garam masala
¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper
salt to taste
¼ cup vegetable oil for frying

Step 1 Place the potatoes, carrot, green beans, and peas in a pot with enough water to cover. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer 15 minutes, until potatoes are tender. Drain vegetables, transfer to a bowl, and mash with a potato masher, leaving a few small chunks.
Step 2 Mix the egg, 3/4 cup bread crumbs, garam masala, cayenne pepper, and salt into the bowl with the mashed vegetables. Form the mixture into small patties about 1/2 inch thick. Dredge the patties in the remaining bread crumbs to coat.
Step 3 Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium heat, and fry the coated patties until golden brown. Drain on paper towels.
