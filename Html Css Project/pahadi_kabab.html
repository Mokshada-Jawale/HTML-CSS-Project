PAHADI KABAB


Ingrediants

Salt
1/2 Cup Coriander Leaves
1/4 Cup Mint Leaves
3 Green Chilies
2 Tsp Ginger Garlic Paste
1/2 Lemon Juice
Water
1/2 Kg Boneless Chicken
2 Tbsp. Gram Flour (Roasted)
4 Tbsp. Fresh Cream
4 Tbsp. Curd
1 2/2 Tsp Cardamom Powder
1 Tsp Carom Seeds
1 Tsp Cumin Seeds Powder
Garam Masala Powder
Dried Fenugreek Leaves
Oil
Chat Masala
Lemon Juice

Step 1 Take coriander leaves, mint leaves, green chillies, ginger garlic paste, salt, lemon and water and make a paste in a mixer grinder. Pour this paste onto boneless chicken.
Step 2 Add besan, cream, curd, cardamom powder, carom seeds, cumin powder, garam masala and fenugreek seeds. Mix all this together. Add 2 tbsp oil and mix it well. Leave for 1 hour to marinate.
Step 3 Now cook the marinated chicken in a pan by adding oil.
Step 4 After you fry the chicken on the pan, place the chicken on skewers and roast it on gas.
Step 5 Add a little bit of oil or butter while roasting. Roast for 1-2 minutes on 1 side.
Step 6 After the kebabs are roasted, add chaat masala and lemon juice to enhance taste.
Step 7 Chicken pahadi kababs are now ready.
