SWEET-CORN SOUP


Ingrediants


Sweet corn kernels boiled 1/2 cup
Vegetable stock 4 cups
Oil 1 tablespoon
Cabbage 1/4 small
Carrot finely chopped 1/4 medium
Sweet corn ( cream style) 2 cups
White pepper powder 1/2 teaspoon
Salt to taste
Cornflour/ corn starch 3 tablespoons

Step 1 Heat oil in a non stick wok. Grate cabbage and add to the hot oil. Add carrot and saut√© on high heat for 1-2 minutes.
Step 2 Add cream style sweet corn and mix well. Add 4 cups vegetable stock and mix and allow it to come to a boil.
Step 3 Mix pepper powder in 2 tbsps water and add to the soup along with salt and mix well.
Step 4 Add corn kernels and allow it to come to a boil again. Mix cornflour in 2 tbsps water and add to the soup and cook till it thickens. Serve hot.
