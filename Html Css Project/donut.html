DUNKIN DONUTS CHOCOLATE


Ingrediants


2 oz semi-sweet bakerâ€™s chocolate, plus more for garnish
2 tsp chocolate milk powder
2 tsp raw sugar
1/2 tsp cinnamon powder
1 cup milk
4 tbsp whipped cream

1 Crush the chocolate into small chunks in the microwave, melt the chocolate with ten-second pulses until smooth.
2 Add the chocolate powder, sugar and cinnamon powder to the hot chocolate and mix well.
3 Slowly add the milk while whisking until fully incorporated.
4 Let the chocolate mixture cool down.
5 In a blender, add the ice and the chocolate mixture. Blend until smooth.
6 Serve and optionally garnish with whipped cream and chocolate bits.
